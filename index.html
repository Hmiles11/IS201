Daily Spill

Welcome to my blog.

If you're reading this then you probably have nothing better to do. No matter, I'm glad you're here. This is a place where I will share my experiences and stories and I hope it can find some influence in your life. Life is like a movie, which character will you play?

Today

Had to win the day. Start off each day with something that challenges you. My challenge that started my day was waking up early and hitting the gym.

Every day is meant to be lived. Our minds and bodies desire to be challenged. We need to be actors and not acted upon.

Work Tasks

I have the best job in the world. I leave town for a couple months during the summer and I sell pest control. This job has given me great opportunities. It also challenges me each day.

I had a couple meetings for work scheduled today. Some of these were a few phone calls with people who are interested in joining the team. There are a selected few who choose to experience this job. I also had a couple trainings I had to do with some guys from the team so that filled up a good portion of my day.

School Tasks

Life as a student at BYU is super busy. We are just about to enter finals week which makes life even more hectic. I had some homework I had to finish up in a few classes. I'm not sure what major I want to get so most of my classes are just generals.

Daily Thoughts

A thought that seemed valuable to share was the importance of using time wisely. Something I believe many of us struggle with is waisting time. Time is a valuable thing. There are only so many minutes in a day and if we choose to waste them, we can never get them back.

That is something I am trying to change in my life. Living more intentionally. That is one of the reasons I started this blog. To share my experiences with the world and to also recap my day by typing it on "The Daily Spill." If I have nothing good to write about it might mean I have waisted the day. No more days will be waisted.
